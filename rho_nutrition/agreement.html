Section 01

Scope of Work

Andrea Palacio ("Provider") agrees to design, build, and deliver the following two systems for Rho Nutrition ("Client"):

  • Zendesk AI Support Agent — An AI system integrated with Zendesk, Shopify, and Retextion that pulls full customer context (order history, subscription status, past interactions) and drafts personalized responses for each ticket. Includes escalation logic to route tickets to human agents when appropriate.
  • 24/7 Voice Agent — An AI phone agent trained on the Client's macro library, product catalog, and business rules, capable of handling Tier 1 customer calls with natural language responses and intelligent escalation to human agents.

This agreement covers only the two systems above. No other services, tools, or systems are included in this scope.

Section 02

Timeline & Delivery

Both systems will be delivered within 30 days of the date the Provider receives all required access and materials from the Client (see Section 5).

Delivery includes a testing period during which the Client's team reviews AI drafts and voice agent responses before either system goes live. The Provider will refine both systems based on team feedback during this period at no additional charge.

Section 03

Payment

The total fee for both systems is a one-time payment of $6,500 USD, due in full upon signing this agreement. Work begins upon receipt of payment.

Section 04

Satisfaction Guarantee

If either system does not meet the Client's reasonable quality standards upon delivery, the Provider will continue refining and improving both systems at no additional charge until the Client is satisfied with the outcome. This commitment has no time limit and is binding on the Provider.

"Satisfied" means the AI response drafts and voice agent responses are accurate, appropriately personalized, and consistent with the Client's existing quality standards for customer communication.

Section 05

Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to provide the following within 5 business days of payment to allow work to begin:

  • Read-only or admin access to Zendesk, Shopify, and Retextion
  • Full Zendesk macro library (exported or shared)
  • A sample of 300+ real customer ticket interactions for training
  • Written refund policy, cancellation policy, and escalation rules
  • Full product catalog with descriptions, FAQs, and key product details
  • One designated contact on the customer service team to provide feedback during the testing period

Delays in providing the above materials will extend the delivery timeline accordingly. The 30-day clock starts from the date all materials are received.

Section 06

Handover & Documentation

Upon completion and Client approval of both systems, the Provider will deliver a full Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) document covering:

  • How to update and expand the AI training data over time
  • How to adjust escalation rules and response logic
  • How to add new macros and product information
  • How to monitor performance and identify areas for improvement
  • Recommended practices for getting the best results from both systems

The Client's team will be able to manage both systems independently after handover using this documentation.

Section 07

Ownership & Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of full payment, all custom systems, workflows, integrations, and documentation built for the Client under this agreement become the sole property of Rho Nutrition. The Provider retains no ownership over any deliverable produced under this agreement.

Third-party tools and platforms used in the build (Zendesk, Shopify, Retextion, etc.) remain subject to their own terms of service.

Section 08

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any non-public business information shared during this engagement. The Provider will not share, disclose, or use any Client data, customer information, or business processes for any purpose outside of delivering the services described in this agreement.

Section 09

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with written notice. If the Client terminates before delivery, the $6,500 setup fee is non-refundable for work already completed. If the Provider is unable to deliver the agreed systems, a full refund will be issued.

The monthly retainer (if active) may be cancelled at any time with 7 days' written notice, with no penalties or fees.

Section 10

Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through good-faith negotiation between the parties.
